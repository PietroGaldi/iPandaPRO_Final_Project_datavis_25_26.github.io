The RAISE Ecosystem:
Bridging Research and Society

RAISE (Robotics and AI for Socio-economic Empowerment) is a strategic Innovation Ecosystem based in Liguria, funded by the Italian Ministry of University and Research (MUR).

Leveraging the region's status as a "Robot Valley," the project unites UNIGE, CNR, and IIT to transfer advanced Robotics and AI solutions from labs to the market.

The Word Cloud highlights the dominant research topics driving this mission.

Innovation Pulse

This platform visualizes the scientific footprint of the ecosystem. By analyzing the flow of knowledge production (2022-2025), we can uncover how research is disseminated.

The ecosystem produces a diverse range of outputs, from Journal Articles to Conference Papers, creating a dynamic flow of knowledge between academia and industry.
