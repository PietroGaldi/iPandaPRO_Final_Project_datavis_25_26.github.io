Synergies & Connectivity

This section provides an analysis of the scientific collaborations within the RAISE project. The objective is to visualize the ecosystem's connectivity across two key dimensions: interpersonal co-authorship networks and institutional partnerships.

Collaborative Dynamics

How are the researchers involved in RAISE connected? This interactive network graph visualizes the co-authorship relations between individuals. Through the search tools it's possible to map networks for specific researchers or highlight specific institutions to see how teams share ideas. Dense clusters indicate strong working groups, while bridging nodes represent key connectors in the ecosystem.
For a more granular perspective, the visualization allows you to center on a single author. This reveals their unique web of collaborators and maps their specific publications, highlighting the individual's personal footprint within the wider ecosystem.

Select one or more institutions to color nodes.

Institutional Synergies

This map makes visible the worldwide architecture of RAISE network. It aggregates co-authorship data to visualize institutional collaborations across the globe. The thickness of the lines corresponds to the intensity of joint research output, highlighting relationships between institutions. Navigating through different regions, it's possible to see how local institutes integrate with the global scientific community.
Above all, the map underscores the central role of Liguria. As the project's origin, it anchors the ecosystem, connecting local research with the wider world.
