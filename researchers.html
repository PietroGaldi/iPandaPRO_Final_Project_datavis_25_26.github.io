The Human Element of RAISE

This section provides an analysis of the researchers involved in the RAISE project. The objective is to visualize the workforce distribution across three key dimensions: institutional affiliation, geographic location, and thematic research expertise.

Mapping Research Volume

The RAISE ecosystem is built on strong institutional pillars. This bubble chart visualizes the volume of contributions per researcher, showing their institutional affiliations . Larger bubbles indicate a higher number of registered publications, highlighting the key contributors within each organization.

Locating the Workforce

Explore the physical distribution of the RAISE community. This view organizes researchers by their geographic base, allowing to filter through different territories. It provides an immediate snapshot of where the workforce is located and the global reach of the RAISE project.

Researchers and Experitse

Beyond affiliation and location, what is the focus of research for the people in RAISE? The Leaderboard ranks researchers by quantity and by thematic focus of their publications. Utilizing semantic analysis of publication topics, this tool categorizes experts into primary domains (e.g., Computer Science, Engineering) and visualizes their specific focus topics, facilitating the identification of specific experts.
