Institutions in the RAISE ecosystem

This section provides an analysis of the institutions participating in the RAISE ecosystem. The objective is to visualize the organizational landscape across key dimensions: geographic distribution, temporal participation, and institutional prominence.

Global Institutional Reach

The ecosystem extends far beyond its local roots. This map visualizes the worldwide geographic distribution of all institutions affiliated with the project's scientific publications. By highlighting the density of distinct institutions per country, it reveals the true global scale of the research collaborations generated by the project.
The global nature of the participation is evident, yet the density of European and particularly Italian institutions, stands out significantly.

Ranking Institutional Contributions

Who is driving the scientific production? This visualization ranks organizations by the volume of publications they have signed. Reflecting the project's evolution over time, it offers a dynamic view of the ecosystem's activity. This approach captures the full spectrum of contributors, identifying not only the core RAISE members but also the universities and research centers collaborating on the project's scientific output.
